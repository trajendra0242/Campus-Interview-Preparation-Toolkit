ABOUT CampusInterviewPrepToolkitTM

CampusInterviewPrepToolkit is built on the premise that practice & preparation are crucial to get over your anxieties when attending an interview.

As the adage goes – practice makes perfect! Video-Based interviews by CampusInterviewPrepToolkit giveS you the platform to prepare, practice and experience firsthand how a real-life job interview feels. Familiarizing yourself with the interview environment beforehand in a relaxed and stress-free environment gives you an edge over your peers.


Interviews are crucial moments in one’s career. Theoretical knowledge of interview questions isn't enough when you actually face an interview. Too often fear takes over our performance in job interviews.

Our Video--Based interviews are conducted by handpicked college experts with an average experience of 9+ years in their interviewing game. So you’re sure to improve your chances of getting hired!
